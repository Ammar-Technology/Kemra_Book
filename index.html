Special Offers

Early Booking Offer

Customers who book their journey 2–4 weeks in advance receive a special discount. This offer encourages early planning and guarantees availability for preferred routes.

Group & Family Package

A special package designed for families and groups where customers can book together and benefit from a free or discounted seat (e.g., book 4 and get 1 free). This creates a more social and affordable travel experience.

Seasonal & Holiday Offer

Special offers during peak seasons such as winter, Eid, and New Year. Customers receive added value such as free meals or discounted prices, enhancing their overall experience during holidays.

Loyalty Program (Kemra Rewards)

Customers earn reward points with every booking that can later be redeemed for discounts, free trips, or service upgrades. This encourages repeat usage and builds long-term customer relationships.

First-Time Customer Offer

A special discount for new customers using Kemra Mobile Hotel for the first time, helping attract new users and reduce hesitation in trying the service.

Referral Offer

Customers who refer friends or family receive discounts or benefits once the referred person completes a booking. This increases customer acquisition through word-of-mouth marketing.

Weekday / Weekend Offer

Discounts are provided on low-demand days (weekdays) or special packages for weekends to balance demand and increase occupancy rates.

Luxury Upgrade Offer

Customers are offered the opportunity to upgrade to premium services (VIP seating, enhanced rooms, exclusive dining) at a reduced price, increasing perceived value.

Birthday & Special Occasion Offer

Customers celebrating birthdays or special occasions receive complimentary items such as cake, decorations, or personalized services, creating a memorable emotional experience.

Limited-Time Flash Offer

Short-term promotional discounts announced on social media for a limited time (e.g., 24 hours), creating urgency and encouraging quick booking decisions.

Off-Peak Offer

Discounted prices during low-demand periods to ensure continuous bookings and maximize utilization of the mobile hotel.

All-Inclusive Package

A bundled offer that includes accommodation, meals, entertainment, and services at a reduced combined price, providing convenience and higher perceived value.
